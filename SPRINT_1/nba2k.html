NBA 2k25 (PS5, XBOX Series X, PC)

NBA 2K25 offers players the chance to pursue their NBA dreams through immersive gameplay modes such as MyCAREER and MyNBA, where you can lead a dynasty or build one as a General Manager. The game features authentic single-player and multiplayer experiences, allowing players to stack wins and make history with enhanced controls and gameplay.

Game details

Release date: September 6, 2024

Developer: Visual Concepts

Publisher: 2K

Game Modes in NBA 2K25

MyCAREER

Pay homage to legends of the game in MyCAREER as you eclipse their milestone achievements and usher in the greatest dynasty the sport has ever seen. The City (New Gen) is the ultimate proving ground; hit the park for some competitive streetball fun and compete at various new venues. Let your talents shine in a bustling City and etch your name among the greats.

myTEAM

Competition thrives in MyTEAM. Challenge rival lineups with your all-time MyTEAM roster, and clash with other elite players in the weekly King of the Court. Go head-to-head and showcase why you deserve to be crowned the best of the best. Collect new cards, master the fundamentals, and earn great rewards. The goal is winning, but where will you start?

SEASONS

NBA 2K25 features exciting earnable rewards and consumable items, along with all-new content, every six weeks via Seasons. Compete in MyTEAM and MyCAREER to accrue XP and start leveling up the Seasonal rewards ladder to earn and collect thematic rewards, from MyTEAM cards to skill boosts, and more. Each Season offers 40 levels of rewards, and an additional 40 levels of bonus content with the Pro Pass and Hall of Fame Pass*.

*NBA 2K25 Pro Passes and Hall of Fame passes are available for separate purchase and require base game, internet connection and NBA 2K account. Paid premium passes unlock additional rewards for the season that are earnable through gameplay. Progress and rewards reset at the end of each season.
