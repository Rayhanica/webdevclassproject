About Just Cause 4

Just Cause 4 is an open-world action-adventure game that brings you into the chaotic world of Rico Rodriguez. Set in the fictional South American world of Solis, the game offers extreme weather, explosive action, and limitless opportunities for mayhem as you take down a powerful dictatorship.
